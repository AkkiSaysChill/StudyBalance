Who we are

We are a small team of students, learning developers who understand how hard it is to juggle classes, coaching, and self‑study without sacrificing health.

Our app uses intelligent scheduling to divide your day into deep‑work sessions, short breaks, and dedicated exercise time, so you can study smarter and still sleep well, move your body, and recharge your mind.

  • Automatically generated study plans based on subjects, deadlines, and difficulty.
  • Built‑in rest and workout blocks to protect your health and focus.
  • Clear daily timetable so you always know what to study and when.
